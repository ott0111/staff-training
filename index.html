Ticket Types

General Ticket

Created by community members for support inquiries, moderator assistance, or reporting issues. Handle with professionalism and follow escalation procedures for complex matters.

Roster Ticket

Application channel for users seeking to join Void Esports. Initial response must include age verification and direction to appropriate resources. Follow structured onboarding workflow.

Protocol: All ticket responses must begin with a professional greeting and age inquiry. Direct applicants properly and maintain detailed records of all interactions.

Moderator Responsibilities & Commands

Warn / Report

Use *warn user-id reason in #staff-commands for minor violations. Use /report for major or repeated infractions with documentation.

LOA

Use /loa when unavailable. Include duration and reason. Extended absences require senior staff notice.

Shift Management

Start with /login and end with /logout. Missing logs may cause disciplinary review.
